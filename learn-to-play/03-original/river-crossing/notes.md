# River Crossing

**Provenance:** Original composition by Heifer Zephyr for this kora build.
MIT licensed — free to perform, record, and arrange.

**Designed for:** Tony Koop's kora (Tomora Bara tuning, F pentatonic).

**Range:** F3–G5. Uses both bridge rows throughout.

**Form:** AABB.

**Character:** A flowing, meditative piece — the title evokes the moment of
hesitation before stepping into a river current, then the decision to cross.

**Practice tips:**
- The A section alternates steadily between left and right bridge strings.
  Feel the shimmer as the two rows interlock.
- The B section has a longer arc — the melody rises to G5 in bar 14 before
  resolving back down to F3.
- At ♩=84 this should breathe naturally. Don't rush the long F8 at the end
  of each section — it's the river, not the crossing.
