# F Pentatonic Scale Walk

**Provenance:** Original exercise.

**Scale:** F major pentatonic (F A C F) + G major pentatonic colour (G Bb D G).
Together these two rows cover the full standard Tomora Bara tuning palette.

**How to play:**
- First section (bars 1–2): right-hand index finger walks up the left bridge
  strings F–A–C–F, then back down. Mirror with right bridge G–Bb–D–G in bars 3–4.
- Second section: practice the interlock — alternate left-bridge (F–A–C) and
  right-bridge (G) strings on each beat.

**Practice tips:**
- The kora characteristic sound comes from the *alternation* between the two
  string rows. Even at warmup speed, try to feel the left–right–left–right
  hand exchange.
- Keep your grip handles loose — tension in the grip tightens the pluck and
  kills resonance.
