Design Summary

The workbook indicates a 20.3 in diameter, 9.8 in deep segmented bowl, a 51.2 in neck, string lengths from 8.7 to 31.1 in, and a 21-string Mersenne-Taylor schedule. The segmented wooden bowl is a prototype substitute, not a traditional-material claim.

Shop Hold Points
